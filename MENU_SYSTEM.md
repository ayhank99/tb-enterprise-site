## 🎨 Tasarım Özellikleri

### Renkler
- Primary: Green-700 (#15803d)
- Background: White
- Text: Stone-900, Stone-700
- Hover: Green-800

### Animasyonlar
- Duration: 200ms
- Easing: Default cubic-bezier
- Fade ve slide efektleri

### Responsive Breakpoints
- Mobile: < 1024px (accordion menü)
- Desktop: ≥ 1024px (dropdown menü)
